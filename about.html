About Mirinda

"The call you've been putting off? This is it."

LCSW-C Certified Trauma Professional Licensed Clinical Psychotherapist C-PTSD Specialist Eating Disorders Family Estrangement Chronic Illness Queer Affirming DC Metro Area

Hi, I'm Mirinda.

I've been practicing since 2022, and in that time I've had the privilege of working with dozens of the most resilient people I've ever met. My clinical work is rooted in complex trauma (C-PTSD), eating disorders, and family estrangement, and I have extensive training supporting alienated populations.

I grew up navigating the kind of childhood that doesn't leave you easily. My own experiences as a granddaughter of an immigrant family, coupled with parental substance use and abandonment, shaped me in ways I'm still learning to understand. I know what it feels like to need support and not know where to find it. I know how hard it is to ask for help, how overwhelming the mental healthcare system can be, and how absolutely life-changing it is when you finally find the right person.

That's why I started Call Your Therapist. Not just to offer services — but to be a real starting point. Someone who can help you make sense of what you're experiencing, point you toward the right resources, and walk alongside you as you figure out your next step.

I also see individual clients through my private practice and am licensed in DC, MD, and VA.

I started Call Your Therapist because I wanted to be the person I needed when I was younger — someone warm, knowledgeable, and real. Someone who doesn't just point you in a direction but actually helps you get there.
