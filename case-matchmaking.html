Case Match-Making

Not sure where to start? That's the whole point.

The mental healthcare system is overwhelming — even for people who work in it. Case Match-Making is a service designed to cut through the noise and connect you with the right support, the right provider, and the right path forward. Think of it as having a knowledgeable professional in your corner who actually knows how the system works.

✦ What Is It?

More than a referral. A real match.

Case Match-Making is a personalized service where I take the time to understand your situation — your history, your needs, your preferences, your insurance — and find providers, programs, or resources that are actually a good fit for you.

This isn't a Google search or a generic list of names. It's a thoughtful, human process guided by someone with clinical training and real-world experience navigating the system. Whether you're looking for a therapist, a psychiatrist, an intensive outpatient program, or just don't know what kind of support you need yet — I can help you figure it out.

✦ it's a bridge to get you there ✦

For Individuals

✦You've been putting off finding a therapist because you don't know where to start. Let me start for you.

For Parents

✦Your child needs support and the system feels impossible. I'll help you navigate it with clarity and care.

For Loved Ones

✦Someone you care about needs help and you don't know how to help them get it. I do.

For Anyone

✦You know you need something but you're not sure what. That's a perfectly valid place to start.

✦ How It Works

Your story matters. So does your match.

Case Match-Making isn't a quick form or a generic referral list. It's a real conversation — one where I take the time to actually understand who you are and what you need before making any recommendations.

01

We Meet

We start with a session where you share your story. Your background, your history, what you've tried before, what hasn't worked — and most importantly, what you're hoping to find. There's no rush and no judgment.

02

We Get Clear on Your Goals

Together, we clarify what you're actually looking for. Whether that's a therapist who specializes in trauma, a psychiatrist who takes your insurance, a support group, a referral for a good primary doctor, or something else entirely — we get specific about what a good match looks like for you.

03

I Find Your Match

Using my clinical background and knowledge of the healthcare landscape, I research and identify providers, programs, or resources that genuinely fit your needs. Not just anyone — the right ones. Whether it's therapy, psychiatry, primary care, or another healthcare concern, I help bridge the gap between you and the support you deserve.

Ready to find your match?

Sessions are $50 and available as a one-time or short-term engagement. Reach out to get started — no wrong reason to reach out.
