The Heart Behind It

Not therapy. But a pretty great place to start.

Call Your Therapist exists because sometimes you need more than an answer — you need someone who truly gets it.

I'm a licensed therapist and certified trauma professional with experience in outpatient mental health treatment. But beyond my credentials, I bring something that can't be taught: lived experience across multiple levels of mental healthcare. I know what it's like to need support, to navigate a system that can feel overwhelming, and to come out the other side.

I started Call Your Therapist to be the resource I wish I'd had — for fellow therapists navigating complex cases, for organizations that want to genuinely support their people, and for anyone looking to better understand their own mental health. Whether that looks like professional consultation, workplace training, psychoeducational resources, or helping you find the right therapist or healthcare provider, everything I offer is grounded in both clinical expertise and real human experience.

Because good mental health support shouldn't be hard to find.

About Mirinda

Hi, I'm Mirinda Keane —
I do this because I've been there.

I've been practicing since 2022, and in that time I've had the privilege of working with some of the most resilient people I've ever met. My clinical work is rooted in complex trauma (C-PTSD), eating disorders, and family estrangement, and I have extensive training supporting alienated populations.

But honestly, my work didn't start in a classroom — it started long before that, in my own life. I grew up navigating complex trauma, family estrangement, and a whole lot of situations I didn't have the language for at the time. I know what it feels like to need support and not know where to find it. I know how hard it is to ask for help, how overwhelming the mental health system can be, and how absolutely life-changing it is when you finally find the right person in your corner.

I started Call Your Therapist because I wanted to be the person I needed when I was younger — someone warm, knowledgeable, and real. Someone who doesn't just point you in a direction but actually helps you get there.

I'm based in the DC metro area and work with individuals, fellow clinicians, parents, and organizations who are ready to take mental health seriously. Whatever brought you here — I'm glad you found me.
